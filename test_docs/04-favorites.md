# Favorites

**What it does:** Farmers can save interesting equipment to a favourites list for quick access later.

---

## Prerequisites

- A **Farmer** account
- At least one active listing from an Owner

---

## 1. Add a Favorite

1. Log in as **Farmer**
2. Open any equipment detail page
3. Click the **heart icon** (toggle button)
4. **Expected:** Heart fills with colour — equipment is now saved

## 2. View Favorites

1. Go to `/farmer/favorites` or click "Favorites" in the navigation
2. **Expected:** You see all equipment you've favourited, with photo, title, and rate

## 3. Remove a Favorite

1. On the favorites page, click the heart icon on any item
2. **Expected:** The item disappears from the list
3. (The heart on the detail page also returns to unfilled state)

## Edge Cases

- **Double-tap:** Clicking the heart repeatedly toggles without errors
- **No favourites yet:** The page shows an empty state with a "Browse equipment" link
- **Favourited equipment gets deleted:** The listing shows as "Unavailable" on your favourites page
